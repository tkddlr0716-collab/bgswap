If you sell on Amazon, Etsy, or Shopify, you've probably spent too much time editing product photo backgrounds. Three popular tools claim to solve this — but they take very different approaches.

**remove.bg** — Background removal specialist. Upload a photo, get a transparent PNG. That's it. You add your own background afterward.

**PhotoRoom** — Full photo editing suite. Background removal is one feature among many: AI backgrounds, virtual models, templates, batch editing. It's a Swiss army knife.

**BgSwap** — Background removal + replacement in one step. Upload a product photo, get 15 marketplace-ready backgrounds with drop shadows. Focused on e-commerce sellers.

## When to Use Which

### Choose remove.bg if:
- You only need background **removal** (not replacement)
- You have your own editing workflow and just need transparent PNGs
- You process images occasionally (pay-as-you-go is convenient)

### Choose PhotoRoom if:
- You need a **full photo editing suite**, not just backgrounds
- You want AI-generated lifestyle backgrounds, virtual models, or templates
- You're willing to pay a monthly subscription
- You need advanced features like ghost mannequin or product staging
- You process high volumes (5,000+ images/month)

### Choose BgSwap if:
- You want **background removal + replacement done in one step**
- You sell on Amazon, Etsy, or Shopify and need marketplace-compliant images
- You want **15 ready-to-use backgrounds** without any editing
- You prefer a **one-time payment** over subscriptions
- You need 10–100 products processed (sweet spot for Starter/Pro)

---

## Where BgSwap Falls Short (Honest Take)

- **No custom backgrounds.** You get 15 preset colors. If you need a lifestyle scene or branded background, PhotoRoom is better.
- **No API.** If you need to integrate background processing into your own system, remove.bg or PhotoRoom have APIs.
- **No mobile app.** BgSwap works in mobile browsers, but PhotoRoom has a dedicated app with more features.
- **No templates or editing tools.** BgSwap does one thing. If you need text overlays, resizing, or social media templates, look elsewhere.
- **Scale limits.** The Pro plan handles 100 products. For 1,000+ products/month, remove.bg's subscription or PhotoRoom's Ultra plan is better suited.

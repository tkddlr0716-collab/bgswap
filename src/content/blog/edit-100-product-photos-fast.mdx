## The 15-Minute Workflow

This is the actual process, not a theoretical one. Timed with 100 real product photos.

### Step 1: Prepare Your Images (2 minutes)

Gather all 100 product photos into one folder. They don't need to be perfect:
- Phone photos work fine
- Supplier images work fine
- Mixed sizes and formats are OK
- Don't waste time pre-editing — the AI handles messy backgrounds

The only thing that matters: the product should be clearly visible in the photo.

### Step 2: Upload (3 minutes)

Go to [BgSwap](https://bgswap.io). After your free preview and purchase:

1. Select all 100 images from your folder
2. Upload starts immediately
3. Progress bar shows upload status

100 images at standard resolution typically upload in 2-3 minutes on a normal connection.

### Step 3: Processing (10 minutes)

This is the hands-off part. BgSwap processes 3 images in parallel:

- AI removes each background
- Each product is placed on 15 different backgrounds (white, dark, marble, gradients, textures)
- Drop shadows are added automatically
- You can close the tab — processing continues on the server

**100 products × 15 backgrounds = 1,500 images generated automatically.**

You'll get an email when everything is done.

### Step 4: Download (1 minute)

Click the download link. Get a ZIP file with all 1,500 images, organized by product.

Total time actively working: about 5 minutes. Total wall-clock time: about 15 minutes.

## When This Workflow Breaks Down

Let's be honest about limitations:

- **Transparent products** (clear glass, acrylic): AI accuracy drops. You may need to re-shoot on a contrasting background.
- **Very small products** (jewelry details, watch faces): Higher resolution source photos give better results.
- **Products held by hands**: The AI may include or exclude hands inconsistently. Use photos without hands for best results.

For 90%+ of standard e-commerce products (boxes, bags, electronics, clothing, toys, kitchenware), the workflow above produces marketplace-ready results.

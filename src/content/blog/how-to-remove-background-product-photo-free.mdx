Whether you're listing on Amazon, Etsy, or Shopify, clean product photos sell more. The biggest difference between amateur and professional product photos? The background.

Here's how to remove backgrounds from product photos for free — and which method actually works for sellers with more than a handful of products.

---

## Why Background Removal Matters

- **Amazon requires pure white (#FFFFFF)** for main images. Off-white = listing suppressed.
- **Etsy and Shopify** don't mandate white, but clean backgrounds increase click-through rates by 20-40%.
- **Consistency** across listings looks professional and builds trust.

---

## Method 1: Free Online Tools (One Photo at a Time)

### remove.bg

The most popular free background remover. Upload a photo, get a transparent PNG.

- **Free tier:** 1 download per image (low-res, 625×400)
- **HD:** Requires credits ($0.23-0.79 per image)
- **Limitation:** Removes background only — you still need to add a new background manually

### Canva Background Remover

Built into Canva's editor.

- **Free tier:** Not available (Pro only, $12.99/month)
- **Quality:** Good for simple products
- **Limitation:** Subscription required

### Adobe Express

Free background removal from Adobe.

- **Free tier:** Yes, with watermark limitations
- **Quality:** Good
- **Limitation:** One photo at a time, no batch

### Verdict

These tools work for 1-5 photos. If you have 10+ products, you'll spend hours uploading one by one, downloading, then adding backgrounds separately.

---

## Method 2: Photoshop (Manual)

The gold standard for quality, but the slowest method.

1. Open image in Photoshop
2. Select Subject (Select → Subject)
3. Refine Edge (Select → Select and Mask)
4. Delete background
5. Add new background layer
6. Export

- **Time:** 10-20 minutes per product
- **Cost:** $22.99/month (Adobe Photography Plan)
- **Quality:** Best, if you have the skills
- **Limitation:** Not scalable. 100 products = 20-30 hours.

## Method 3: Phone Apps

### Photoroom (iOS/Android)

- **Free tier:** 250 exports/month, watermarked
- **Pro:** $12.99/month
- **Quality:** Very good for phone photos
- **Batch:** Limited (Pro only)

### remove.bg App

- Same as web version, on your phone
- Free low-res downloads

### Verdict

Good for sellers who shoot on phone and want quick edits. Not practical for bulk editing.

## Method 5: DIY White Background Setup

If you want to avoid any editing entirely:

1. **White poster board** ($3 from any craft store)
2. **Natural light** from a window
3. **Phone camera** — modern phones are more than enough
4. **Curved backdrop** — bend the poster board to eliminate the horizon line

This gives you a mostly-white background, but you'll still need minor cleanup for pure white (#FFFFFF) compliance on Amazon.

## What Each Tool Does

**remove.bg** — Background removal specialist. Upload a photo, get a transparent PNG. That's it. You add your own background afterward in Photoshop, Canva, or another tool.

**PhotoRoom** — Full photo editing suite. Background removal is one of many features: AI-generated backgrounds, virtual models, ghost mannequin, 1000+ templates, batch editing. Think of it as an all-in-one editing platform.

**BgSwap** — Background removal + replacement in one step. Upload a product photo, get 15 marketplace-ready backgrounds (solids, gradients, and textures) with optional drop shadows. No editing needed. Focused specifically on e-commerce sellers who need clean product photos fast.

## When to Use Which

### Choose remove.bg if:
- You only need background **removal** (not replacement)
- You have your own editing workflow and just need transparent PNGs
- You process images occasionally and pay-as-you-go is convenient
- You need API access for integration into your own tools

### Choose PhotoRoom if:
- You need a **full photo editing suite**, not just backgrounds
- You want AI-generated lifestyle backgrounds, virtual models, or templates
- You're willing to pay a monthly subscription for ongoing access
- You need advanced features like ghost mannequin or product staging
- You process high volumes (5,000+ images/month)

### Choose BgSwap if:
- You want **background removal + replacement done in one step**
- You sell on Amazon, Etsy, or Shopify and need marketplace-compliant images
- You want **15 ready-to-use backgrounds** without any editing
- You prefer a **one-time payment** over monthly subscriptions
- You need marketplace-specific output sizes (Amazon 2000px, etc.)
- You want control over shadows and product fill percentage
- You process 10–100 products at a time

## Where BgSwap Falls Short (Honest Take)

No tool is perfect. BgSwap doesn't compete in these areas:

- **No AI scene backgrounds.** You get solid colors, gradients, and textures (marble, wood, linen, concrete, paper). If you need AI-generated lifestyle scenes (kitchen counter, living room), PhotoRoom is better.
- **No API.** If you need to integrate background processing into your own system, remove.bg or PhotoRoom have APIs.
- **No mobile app.** BgSwap works in mobile browsers, but PhotoRoom has a dedicated app with more features.
- **No templates or editing tools.** BgSwap does one thing. Text overlays, resizing, social media templates — look elsewhere.
- **Lower max resolution.** BgSwap outputs at 2048px. remove.bg supports up to 25MP (~5000×5000). If you need the absolute highest resolution, remove.bg wins here.
- **Scale limits.** The Pro plan handles 100 products. For 1,000+ products/month, remove.bg's subscription or PhotoRoom's Enterprise plan is better suited.
- **Newer service.** remove.bg and PhotoRoom have years of track record. BgSwap is new.
